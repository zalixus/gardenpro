About Us

The GardenPro Story

GardenPro India is a Bengaluru-based landscape and garden design company serving clients across India. We specialize in transforming outdoor and indoor spaces — from sprawling residential landscapes to compact urban gardens, office green walls, terrariums, and exotic plant collections. Our team of horticulturists and landscape designers believe that every space deserves a connection to nature.

Whether you are looking for a complete garden makeover, a low-maintenance solution for your balcony, a statement terrarium for your living room, or rare exotic fruit plants for your orchard — GardenPro has the expertise and passion to bring it to life. We serve residential clients, corporate offices, resorts, restaurants, and anyone who believes that plants make everything better. Visit us at gardenpro.in.

FAQ

Common Questions

What services does GardenPro India offer? +
GardenPro India offers Landscape Design, Garden Design & Set Up, Garden Maintenance, Exotic Fruits plants, Terrariums, Aqua Terrariums, and Temple Tree — a green co-working space launching soon.
Do you design gardens for homes and offices? +
Absolutely. We design and set up gardens for residential homes, apartments, corporate offices, resorts, restaurants, and commercial properties across India.
Where is GardenPro located and which areas do you serve? +
GardenPro is headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka. We serve clients across India and can consult remotely for design and plant sourcing projects.
What is Temple Tree by GardenPro? +
Temple Tree is our upcoming green co-working space — a nature-immersive workspace surrounded by living plants, natural materials, and biophilic design. It's designed for professionals who believe a green environment boosts creativity and well-being.
Do you sell exotic fruit plants across India? +
Yes! We source and supply rare, tropical, and exotic fruit plants across India. From rambutan and mangosteen to star apple and dragon fruit — contact us to know what's currently available.
Can I get a custom terrarium as a gift? +
Definitely. Our terrariums and aqua terrariums are hand-crafted and make exceptional, long-lasting gifts. We accept custom orders for personal and corporate gifting.
