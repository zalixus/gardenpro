Why It Matters

Plants need more than just water

Bangalore's climate — hot summers, water scarcity, and sudden monsoon humidity — creates very specific challenges for your plants. Without the right care at the right time, even healthy gardens decline quickly.

🌿
Pruning shapes growth

Untrimmed plants become leggy, stop flowering, and block light to other plants. Regular pruning triggers new growth, keeps your garden looking full and well-shaped.

🦟
Pests spread silently

Mealy bugs, aphids and fungal infections start small and spread fast — especially in Bangalore's humidity. Early detection during a visit prevents full garden losses.

🪴
Soil gets depleted

Potted plants in balconies and terraces exhaust nutrients within months. Regular soil loosening, top-dressing and fertiliser application keeps the root zone healthy.

☀️
Sunlight changes seasonally

As the sun's angle shifts through the year, plants that were thriving may start struggling. Our team relocates pots to optimise light exposure each season.

💧
Watering needs guidance

Over-watering is the #1 killer of balcony plants in Bangalore. We calibrate watering schedules for each plant type, season, and pot size — and coach your household help.

📋
You deserve a report

After every visit you receive a WhatsApp photo report — work done, plant health, any concerns. No more wondering what the gardener actually did while you were at work.

The visible, hands-on work — clearing, cutting, and structuring your garden.
