How It Works

Our 3-Step Process

Every garden we build follows a clear, transparent process — no surprises, no delays. You're kept in the loop at every stage via WhatsApp updates.

🌿
1
Concept Consultation

We visit your space — balcony, terrace, villa, or office — and understand your vision, lifestyle, and maintenance capacity. We factor in Bangalore's climate, your sun exposure, water access, and space dimensions to suggest the right plants and design approach. You receive a mood board and plant selection within 48 hours.

Estimate & Quotation

We provide a detailed, itemised quote — plants, pots, structural elements, labour, and any accessories. No hidden charges. We offer three budget tiers so you can choose what works for you. Once you confirm, we lock in a date and procure plants fresh from our trusted nursery sources to ensure quality.

Setup & Installation

Our team arrives on the confirmed date and completes the setup — typically within 1 to 3 days depending on scale. You receive WhatsApp photo updates through the day. Once done, we walk you through care instructions, watering schedules, and connect you to our AMC for ongoing maintenance. Your plants are covered under our 90-day guarantee.
